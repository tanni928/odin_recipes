Peanut Butter Cookies

Classical flaver!

Let's make great cookies together!

Ingredient

  • 2+1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1+1/2 tablespoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 large egg
  • 2 cup crunchy peanut butter

Steps

  1. Cream butter, peanut butter, and sugars together in a bowl; beat in eggs.
  2. In a separate bowl, sift flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt; stir into butter mixture. Put dough in refrigerator for 1 hour.
  3. Roll dough into 1 inch balls and put on baking sheets. Flatten each ball with a fork, making a crisscross pattern.
  4. Bake in a preheated 375 degrees F oven for about 10 minutes or until cookies begin to brown.
